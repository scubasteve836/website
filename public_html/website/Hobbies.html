Hobbies & Interests

Running and Biking

I started running in 8th grade as a sprinter, which is rather ironic because after a year of that I quicking got into distance running which led to a career in cross country. I ran all four years at Lake Forest College and now I still try to run whenever it's possible. I also picked up biking because it was something I could do to stay in shape whenever I couldn't run or needed a day off. I ended up loving biking as a hobby which led to me purchasing my first road bike, Cherry Garcia. Aside from these activities I also dabble in volleyball and I try to swim when I can.

Coffee

Anyone who knows me a little bit probably knows I'm super into coffee. And not Starbucks. This stems from the brief time I worked as a barista for close to a year at a small town roaster, Branch Street Coffee Roasters. Since then I try to make it a point to find the local roaster of any new area I travel to or live in. Chicago has had no shortages of places to try and I go out of my way to get to those shops on the weekends. My favorite coffee is an Ethiopian roast from the shop I used to work at due to its sweetness and notes of blueberries. My favorite espresso is called The Tri-Force which is served at The Wormhole in Wicker park. Of course these are always subject to change.
